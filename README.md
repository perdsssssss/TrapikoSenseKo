🗺️ TrapikoSenseKo

A smart commute assistant for everyday Filipino commuters in Metro Manila.


📌 The Problem
Every day, millions of Filipinos face the same frustrating problem. You're about to leave the house, you check Google Maps, and it tells you your commute will take 45 minutes. But it doesn't tell you what to do about it. It won't tell you that Joyride has a rider nearby, that the MRT is a faster option, or that the jeepney you usually take no longer stops on that street.
Global AI fails here because it doesn't know that Angkas isn't allowed everywhere, that Joyride only covers certain barangays, or that a modernized jeepney no longer stops on your usual route. That hyper-local context is exactly what TrapikoSenseKo provides.

💡 The Solution
TrapikoSenseKo was built for the everyday Filipino commuter who is tired of guessing. When you open the app, you set your destination just like any maps app. But instead of just showing you the traffic, our AI agent actually thinks for you. It looks at the live traffic on your route, checks what transport options are actually available in your area, and tells you exactly what to ride to get there faster and cheaper.

🎯 Target Audience
Everyday Filipino commuters in Metro Manila who rely on public transportation such as jeepneys, MRT, Joyride, Angkas, and Grab.

✨ Features

🗺️ Live Traffic Map — See real-time traffic conditions on your route
🤖 AI-Powered Suggestions — Get smart transport recommendations based on current traffic
🛵 PH Transport Aware — Knows Joyride coverage zones, Angkas restricted areas, MRT capacity, and more
⚡ Instant Results — Set your destination and get a recommendation in seconds
💰 Cost vs. Time Tradeoff — See estimated fare and travel time per transport option
